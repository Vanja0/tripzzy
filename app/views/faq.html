FAQS

What’s Tripzzy?

Tripzzy is a tool for avid travellers seeking RELEVANT travel advice and inspiration from the TRUSTED network of friends, enabling them to plan, organise, share and discover their past and prospective trips.

How does it work?

Users post tripzzy requests on Tripzzy by stating the most important characteristics of their desired trip. We will then encourage their friends to suggest hotels, restaurants, and location content from their own experience. In that way, our users will receive travel advice based on experience of the people they are connected to.

Who can see my tripzzies and travel requests?

Your tripzzy requests are available for your selected Facebook friends to see on Tripzzy.com. They are also available on your Facebook wall if you check "Post on my FB wall" mark when completing a travel request! Your tripzzies are available for all Tripzzters to see on Tripzzy.com! And, if you check the "Post on my FB wall" mark when completing a tripzzy, your tripzzy will appear on your FB wall! Note: If you unfriend someone on Facebook, you will no longer be connected with that person on Tripzzy.com .
